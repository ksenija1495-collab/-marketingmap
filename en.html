👤 Your First Marketing Hire

In 80% of cases, the first hire is a PMM (Product Marketing Manager). Not a performance marketer, not an SMM, not a "jack-of-all-trades marketer."

Why: without packaging (positioning, offer, messaging), any traffic bleeds money. A PMM turns "we have a good product" into "here's exactly why you should buy this."

Exceptions: if conversion already works and you just need more people — then Performance. If it's a long sales cycle with no trust — Content Marketer.

🚩 Hiring Red Flags

⚠️
"I'll increase your reach" — talks vanity metrics, not revenue. Ask: "How does this affect the bottom line?"
⚠️
Doesn't ask about the product or audience — an executor, not a strategist. Good marketers start with questions, not proposals.
⚠️
"Results in 2 weeks" — doesn't understand marketing cycles. Realistic timeline for first results: 2–3 months.
⚠️
Portfolio of only "pretty" projects — ask for numbers: conversion, CAC, ROI. No numbers = no results.
⚠️
"I do everything" — a generalist is fine at early stages. But at stage 2+, you need specialists.

The #1 Mistake

Hiring for the "next stage" before closing the current one:

Running paid traffic without positioning→burning budget
Building a brand without a product→beautiful emptiness
Doing growth without data→guesswork

Simple logic:

Package the messaging→ PMM
Scale the pipeline→ Performance
Retain the revenue→ CRM
Grow systematically→ Growth
Build the system→ CMO

Performance Marketer
Acquisition · paid traffic $5–8K/mo staff · $3–5K project

Required skills

  • Set up and optimize campaigns (Meta, Google, LinkedIn, TikTok)
  • Analytics: UTM, attribution, end-to-end
  • Unit economics: CPL → CAC → ROI
  • Hypothesis testing: creatives, audiences, landing pages
  • Scale without losing efficiency

Problems solved

  • Stable lead flow
  • Reducing CAC
  • Testing new channels
  • Scaling budget with ROI
  • Ad budget management

What to look for

  • Case studies: "reduced CAC from X to Y"
  • Experience with $5K+/mo budgets
  • Understanding of business economics
  • Hypothesis-driven approach
  • Ask: "What will you do in the first 2 weeks?"

Problems solved

  • Organic traffic growth

  • Competitor analysis

What to look for

  • Case studies with organic growth numbers
  • Technical side, not just keywords
  • Experience in a relevant niche
  • Strategic thinking
  • Ask: "What's the first step for our site?"

CRM / Lifecycle Marketer

Required skills

  • RFM segmentation, cohorts
  • CDP: Braze, Customer.io, Klaviyo

Problems solved

What to look for

What to look for

Growth Marketer / Head of Growth
Growth · system & experiments $10–18K/mo staff

Required skills

  • Full funnel: acquisition → retention → revenue → referral
  • Bottleneck analysis
  • Experiment system: hypothesis → test → conclusion
  • Data: SQL, cohorts, LTV modeling
  • Cross-functional management
  • Prioritization: ICE/RICE

Problems solved

  • Growth system (process, not one-off tests)
  • Growth levers → scaling
  • Full-funnel optimization
  • Growth model
  • Product-led growth
  • Experiment backlog

What to look for

  • Cases: "found X → Y% growth"
  • Sees the full funnel, not one channel
  • SQL, BI, not just GA
  • Cross-functional experience
  • Product understanding
  • Ask: "Tell me about a failed experiment"
CMO / VP of Marketing
Architecture · strategy & system $15–30K+/mo staff

Required skills

  • Marketing architecture: channels, products, teams
  • Managing teams of 5–15+
  • Aligning marketing, product, and business goals
  • Marketing P&L
  • Processes: OKRs, planning, reviews
  • Strategy: where to grow, what to kill
  • Market: competitors, trends

Problems solved

  • Chaos → managed system
  • Marketing-product alignment
  • Team building
  • Budgets and forecasting
  • Scaling: markets, products
  • Marketing transformation

What to look for

  • System-building, not just channels
  • "Marketing delivered X revenue"
  • Transformation cases
  • Team management: hiring, processes
  • Strategy → structure → people
  • Ask: "What will you do in the first 90 days?"

  • Copywriter — case studies, proposals, thought leadership

  • Founder / expert personal brand
  • SEO for expert queries
  • Email nurturing (long sequences)

  • Content marketer full-time or project

  • Hiring SMM instead of a content marketer
  • Ignoring founder personal branding
  • Trying to sell directly with a long sales cycle

Subscription services, mobile apps, platforms

Critical roles

  • Growth marketer — product-led growth, metrics, experiments
  • Performance — scaling with clear unit economics
  • CRM / Lifecycle — onboarding, activation, retention

Key focus

  • User activation (onboarding)
  • Retention and churn reduction
  • Product-led growth (freemium, trials)
  • Data and experiments
